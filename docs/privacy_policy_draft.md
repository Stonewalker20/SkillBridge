# Privacy Policy Draft

Draft only. Not legal advice. This document must be reviewed by counsel before public launch.

## Scope

SkillBridge collects and processes account, profile, evidence, job analysis, resume, activity, and operational data to provide career intelligence features.

## What We Collect

- Account data: email, username, role, avatar settings, subscription state, and authentication metadata.
- Profile data: confirmed skills, preferences, personalization settings, and dashboard layout choices.
- User content: evidence submissions, resume snapshots, job descriptions, tailored resume outputs, and uploaded files.
- Usage data: tool actions, recent activity, reward progress, and product analytics.
- Technical data: IP address, device/browser details, cookies, logs, and error traces.

## Why We Collect It

- To authenticate users and maintain sessions.
- To provide evidence ingestion, skill confirmation, job matching, tailored resume generation, and rewards.
- To improve product quality, diagnose failures, prevent abuse, and support billing and account administration.

## How We Use It

- Provide the services the user explicitly requests.
- Store user-generated content so it can be re-used across the app.
- Generate summaries, recommendations, and tailored outputs.
- Maintain product integrity, security, and analytics.

## Sharing

SkillBridge should only share data with:

- Infrastructure and hosting providers.
- Email, analytics, logging, or payment vendors that support the service.
- Legal or compliance authorities if required by law.

No sale of personal information is intended in this draft.

## Retention

Data retention should follow [Data Retention Policy Draft](./data_retention_policy_draft.md). User accounts may remain until deletion or a retention trigger applies.

## User Controls

- Update account details and preferences.
- Delete or deactivate an account where supported.
- Request access to or deletion of stored personal data, subject to legal and operational constraints.

## Security

SkillBridge should use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including access controls, secure transport, and production secret management.

## International Use

If the service is offered outside the United States, this policy should be updated for regional privacy obligations.
